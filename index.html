The Royal Ascension Itinerary

Seven days of adventure, celebration, and relaxation

Description

Welcome to Kenya! Discover the Nairobi National Museum, where Kenya’s rich history, culture, and art come to life. From ancient fossils revealing the story of human origins to vibrant galleries of African art, the museum offers a fascinating glimpse into the heritage and natural beauty of Kenya.

Activities

Morning: Arrivals and hotel check-in’s

Afternoon: Nairobi Museum tour option

Description

Experience the breathtaking Nairobi National Park, the world’s only wildlife park located beside a capital city. Just minutes from downtown Nairobi, the park offers a rare chance to see lions, rhinos, giraffes, and other wildlife roaming freely against the stunning backdrop of the Nairobi skyline. It’s a truly unforgettable blend of nature and city life.

Activities

Morning: Nairobi National Park-Safari Day

Afternoon: Optional Explore Nightlife in Nairobi

Description

Relax at the stunning The Majlis Hotel, a luxury beachfront retreat overlooking the turquoise waters of the Indian Ocean. With elegant Swahili-inspired architecture, serene ocean views, and warm coastal hospitality, it offers the perfect blend of comfort, culture, and tranquility in the heart of Lamu.

Activities

Afternoon: Departure for the Majlis Hotel

Evening: Informal dinner and local exploration.

Description

Celebrate Irede's milestone with wellness activities and an unforgettable evening performance and fireworks.

Activities

Morning: Wellness Morning/Spa

Evening: Irede's 40th Birthday Experience (to end with performances and fireworks).

Description

Celebrate Ibi's special day with a luxurious boat tour and Nikki Beach-style brunch experience.

Activities

Afternoon: Boat Tour to Ibi's 40th Brunch Birthday Experience "Nikki Beach" Style.

Description

Enjoy a variety of local activities in Lamu, from sailing traditional dhows along the Indian Ocean to exploring the charming streets of Lamu Old Town. Experience the island’s unique culture with camel rides on the beach, vibrant local markets, and the laid-back rhythm of coastal Swahili life.

Description

As the celebration comes to an end, take a final moment to soak in the serene beauty of Lamu’s coastline and the gentle rhythm of the Indian Ocean. Depart with unforgettable memories of Kenya’s culture, wildlife, and coastal charm, carrying a piece of this remarkable experience with you.
