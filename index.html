The Royal Ascension Itinerary

Seven days on the east coast of Africa — wildlife, ocean, culture, and the people you love most.

Description

Your adventure begins the moment you touch down. Nairobi is a city of extraordinary contrasts — a skyline rising beside open savannah, a cultural heartbeat unlike anywhere else on the continent. Settle in at the JW Marriott and, when you are ready, begin to explore. The Nairobi National Museum offers a rare window into Kenya's ancient origins and living culture — a fitting first chapter.

Activities

Morning — Arrivals & Check-in at JW Marriott Nairobi

Afternoon — Nairobi National Museum (at your own pace)

Description

This is what Kenya does to you — it shows you something so extraordinary that you cannot quite believe it is real. Nairobi National Park is the only wildlife reserve on earth bordered by a capital city skyline. Lions. Rhinos. Giraffes. All of it, just minutes from where you slept. This morning belongs to the wild. The evening is yours.

Activities

Morning — Safari at Nairobi National Park

Evening — Explore Nairobi at your leisure

Description

Lamu is one of the oldest and best-preserved Swahili settlements in East Africa — a UNESCO World Heritage site where time slows to the rhythm of the Indian Ocean. This afternoon, we depart for The Majlis: a beachfront retreat of Swahili-inspired architecture, warm stone, and turquoise water as far as the eye can see. Tonight, we gather informally for the first time — the celebration begins to take shape.

Activities

Afternoon — Departure for The Majlis Hotel, Lamu.

Evening — Welcome dinner & first evening together.

Description

Today belongs to Irede. The morning opens gently — spa, stillness, the sound of the ocean. The evening rises to meet the moment: a full celebration experience, performances that move you, and fireworks over the Indian Ocean to close a night that will not be forgotten.

Activities

Morning — Wellness & Spa at The Majlis

Evening — Irede's 40th Birthday Experience, with live performances and fireworks

Description

Ibi's birthday is marked with the kind of afternoon that lives in memory long after you have come home, celebration in the style of Nikki Beach: vibrant, joyful, and entirely Ibi.

Activities

Afternoon into Evening — Ibi's 40th Birthday Brunch Experience

Description

There are no obligations today — only possibilities. Sail a traditional dhow along the shoreline as the sun catches the water. Wander the ancient lanes of Lamu Old Town, where donkeys outnumber cars and every doorway tells a story. Ride a camel on the beach at golden hour. Browse the markets. Find a quiet corner of The Majlis and do absolutely nothing at all. This day is yours.

Activities

Dhow sailing / Lamu Old Town / Camel rides / Local markets / Rest at The Majlis

Description

All good things are savoured, not rushed. Take one last morning on the Lamu coast — the light on the water, the warmth of the stone, the people you have celebrated with. We leave Kenya changed, as it always leaves you. Until next time.
